Apply for permission to use our land

Use this service to apply for permission do something on our land, such as:

  • organising an event
  • filming
  • school visits
  • training
  • doing a survey

If you're not sure whether you need to apply for permission or not, you should Check if you're allowed to use our land before you use this service.

Before you start

You'll need:

  • to know the location of the land you want to use
  • to know when you want to use the land
  • to know how many people will attend
  • digital copies of your insurance certificates (if required)
